How to get validated

Start with a mock interview and finish with a job interview!

  • Register with minterview.me

    Submit your contact information, upload your resume and complete registration.

  • Fix an appointment

    Our team will contact you to fix an appointment for you with an interview expert. You can submit your preferred type of interview - technical or HR. Payment information will be mailed to you. Complete the payment to confirm your appointment.

  • Attend the mock interview

    Follow the details on the appointment email to start your interview as per the confirmed schedule. Get a real life interview experience from a leading industry expert. Get skills, profile and candidature validated.

  • Get feedback and guidance

    Receive feedback on your interview performance from the expert - oral and through email. Get professional guidance from the expert to better your performance in actual interviews.
